How It Works & Rules

  • One Item at a Time
    Unlike silent auctions, we sell one item live on the "block". You must be in the Auction Room to bid.
  • Soft Close Timer
    If a bid is placed in the last 30 seconds, the timer resets to 30 seconds to prevent sniping.
  • Winning is Binding
    Winning a bid is a contract to purchase.
  • Local Pickup
    We are a local community. Buyers must arrange pickup with the seller in the Daphne/Fairhope area.
