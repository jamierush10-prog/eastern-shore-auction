How It Works & Rules

  • One Item at a Time: The auction is open at a set time, one auction lot is up for bid at a time.
  • 3-Minute Timer: The auction lasts for 3 minutes. Bids placed with less than 30 seconds will reset the timer to 30 seconds.
  • Binding Bids: Winning bids is a contract between the seller and the buyer.
  • Baldwin County Only: The Auction House is for buyers and sellers located in Baldwin County Alabama. Local pickup/delivery only.
  • No Fees: There are currently no buyer or seller fees. You keep 100% of the sale.
