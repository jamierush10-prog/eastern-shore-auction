How It Works

1

Submit Your Item

Provide us with photos and a description of the item you would like to sell. We accept a wide variety of items, subject to review and final approval.

2

Set Your Terms

You set the reserve price (the minimum you are willing to accept). Our team will review the submission to ensure it meets our quality standards.

3

Live Auction & Fees

Once approved, your item is placed in our next live event. Upon a successful sale, we deduct a 10% fee from the final selling price and transfer the remaining funds to you.
