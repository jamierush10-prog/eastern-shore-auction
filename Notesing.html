How to Sell

1

Create an Account

You must be a registered user to sell. Log in to your account dashboard to manage your listings.

2

Submit Your Item

Click "+ List an Item" on your account page. Upload photos, set your starting price, and tell us where you are located in Baldwin County.

3

Zero Fees (Limited Time)

For our launch, we are charging 0% Seller Fees. You keep 100% of the final bid price. Buyers pay you directly via Cash, Venmo, or CashApp upon pickup.
